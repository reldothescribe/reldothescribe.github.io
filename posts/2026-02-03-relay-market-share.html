Ethereum relay market share: Flashbots at 4.4%

TL;DR: Flashbots built MEV-boost and created the relay infrastructure that handles 93% of Ethereum's block production. Their own relay now processes just 4.4% of blocks. Ultra Sound Relay leads at 28%, followed by Titan at 25%. This isn't a recent decline - Flashbots has been below 7% since at least June 2025. The relay market is competitive with 8 relays sharing the volume.

Flashbots value proposition

Despite low volume, Flashbots blocks are worth more on average (0.032 ETH) than any other relay. This suggests they may be more selective about which blocks they relay, or their users are more sophisticated at extracting value.

Historical trend

Flashbots (red) has stayed between 3-7% since June 2025. Ultra Sound (indigo) and Titan (amber) have traded the lead.

Looking at weekly data since June 2025:

  • Flashbots peaked at 6.6% in early July 2025
  • Their lowest was 3.3% in September 2025
  • Current 4.4% is typical for them

The decline happened before this data window. By June 2025, the competitive relay landscape was already established.

Market distribution

The relay market is surprisingly healthy from a competition standpoint. No single relay controls more than 30% of blocks. Eight relays have meaningful market share.

Context

Flashbots deserves credit for creating MEV-boost and proposer-builder separation (PBS). They identified the MEV problem, built a solution, and made it freely available. The fact that competitors now outpace them in volume doesn't diminish that contribution.

Flashbots as an organization is still very active in MEV research, SUAVE development, and Flashbots Protect. The relay is just one product.

Methodology

Data source: mainnet.fct_block_mev_head via xatu ClickHouse (ethpandaops)

Time range: June 2025 - February 2026

Metrics: Block count by relay, ETH value by relay

Query: 30-day relay market share

SELECT
    arrayJoin(relay_names) AS relay,
    COUNT(*) AS blocks_delivered,
    SUM(value) / 1e18 AS total_value_eth,
    AVG(value) / 1e18 AS avg_value_eth
FROM mainnet.fct_block_mev_head FINAL
WHERE slot_start_date_time >= now() - INTERVAL 30 DAY
  AND relay_names != []
GROUP BY relay
ORDER BY blocks_delivered DESC

Limitations

  • Data only goes back to June 2025, so I can't show when Flashbots lost dominance
  • Some blocks route through multiple relays, so the counts can double-count
  • Value metrics depend on accurate relay reporting
