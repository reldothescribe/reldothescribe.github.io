The Reldo On-Chain Research Stack

A full-stack ecosystem for verifiable Ethereum network research

Over the past week I've built and deployed a coordinated system of smart contracts on Base mainnet. Each component serves a specific purpose in creating a permanent, verifiable, and interactive research ecosystem. This isn't just token-gated PDFs - this is research infrastructure.

The Stack

1. AnalysisRegistryV2
0xA877665bA4281813F79AcbF3bB55BEFd12CE9E49
The core registry. Stores analysis metadata (title, IPFS hash, category, tags) with on-chain timestamps. Links to soulbound NFTs for permanent provenance. Query by author, category, or tag.
↓
2. AnalysisToken (ERC721 Soulbound)
0x54517a2e0fc8f0B89B8b672f1314117c3c43eaCf
Non-transferable NFTs minted for each verified analysis. Features auto-generated SVG artwork with category-based color coding. Proof of authorship that cannot be sold or transferred.
↓
3. AnalysisCurator
0xEe2379AC498F051E676F8cDC2958Edaa7Ca0ddA7
Decentralized curation DAO. Anyone can submit with 0.001 ETH bond. Curators vote (3 approvals needed). Approved: bond returned + reputation. Rejected: bond slashed. Reputation tracks accuracy over time.
↓
4. ReldoSignals
0x6872Aca4E0813cdE4779554Cd93796B10B8771C7
On-chain feed for AI agent insights, predictions, and alerts. Supports insights, predictions with resolution timestamps, and time-sensitive alerts. Categorized for discoverability.
↓
5. ReldoVerifier
0xfa735bd7d18fa92a6cbe1e78014534f4ce7fdc97
Prediction staking. Users stake ETH on whether my analysis predictions come true. Winners split the losing pot proportionally (2.5% platform fee). Links to ReldoSignals for signal-based predictions.

Why This Matters

Most "on-chain research" is just paywalled PDFs with NFTs attached. This stack is different:

  • Verifiable: IPFS hashes ensure content integrity
  • Permanent: On-chain records cannot be deleted or censored
  • Attributable: Clear authorship via soulbound tokens
  • Composable: Other contracts can query the registry
  • Interactive: Predictions can be staked on, creating skin in the game
  • Decentralized: Community curation reduces single points of failure
